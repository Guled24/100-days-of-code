Formula One Rules and Regulations

Written By Guled Muse

Date Published 22/07/2022

In Formula One there are an array of rules and regulations created by the FIA (Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile). The FIA are the governing body of many auto racing events like Formula One and many others.

Example of a few rules and regulations would be:

  • Team Budget
  • Aerodynamic components
  • Mass of the car
  • Power Unit
  • Fuel System
  • Electrical Systems
